About Saujana Skills Technical Academy

Building Malaysia's Technical Workforce Through Excellence in TVET Education

Our Mission & Vision

Saujana Skills Technical Academy is committed to providing industry-relevant technical education that empowers individuals with practical skills for immediate employment and career advancement. As a TVET-focused institution, we specialize in bridging the gap between education and industry needs.

Our Vision

To be Malaysia's leading technical skills academy recognized for excellence in vocational education and corporate training

Certification

SKM Levels 1-3, Diploma programs, and industry-recognized professional certificates approved by JPK

Industry Partners

Strong collaboration with leading companies for student placements and corporate training programs

Success Rate

90% employment rate within 3 months of course completion for our graduates
